MY SINS

DESCEND INTO THE DEPTHS OF DAMNATION

PRIDE

Excessive belief in one's own abilities that interferes with recognition of the grace of God. The first of the deadly sins and the deadliest of all.

"Pride is the beginning of sin." - Ecclesiasticus 10:15

WRATH

Manifested in the individual who spurns love and opts instead for fury. It is the root of murder and assault.

"The wrath of man does not produce the righteousness of God." - James 1:20

LUST

Intense or unbridled desire, especially for carnal pleasures. Lust corrupts the body and blinds the mind to virtue.

"For all that is in the world—the lust of the flesh, the lust of the eyes—is not from the Father." - 1 John 2:16

GLUTTONY

Overindulgence and overconsumption to the point of waste. The excessive desire to consume more than one requires.

"Their end is destruction, their god is their belly." - Philippians 3:19

GREED

The desire for material wealth or gain while ignoring the realm of the spiritual. The sin of excess.

"For the love of money is a root of all kinds of evil." - 1 Timothy 6:10

ENVY

The desire for others' traits, status, abilities, or situation. The sin that feeds on the accomplishments of others.

"For where envy and self-seeking exist, confusion and every evil thing are there." - James 3:16

SLOTH

Avoidance of physical or spiritual work. The failure to utilize one's talents and gifts.

"Through sloth the roof sinks in, and through indolence the house leaks." - Ecclesiastes 10:18
